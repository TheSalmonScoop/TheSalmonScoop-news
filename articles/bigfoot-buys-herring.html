REEDSPORT, Ore., July 17, 2026 — Customers at a coastal bait counter reported a towering, hair-covered figure purchasing herring, coffee, and a single scratch-off ticket before disappearing toward the tree line.

The figure allegedly pointed at the Umpqua and shook its head slowly, which witnesses interpreted as either disappointment in fishing conditions or commentary on local boat parking.

Authorities have not confirmed the sighting. The receipt, however, included “extra scent,” “two donuts,” and “do not ask questions” written in pencil.

“Whatever it was, it knew exactly which bait cooler to open,” one witness said.
